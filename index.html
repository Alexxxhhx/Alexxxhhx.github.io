Hi. I’m Aleksey Shevchuk.

I'm from Kiev, Ukraine.
I create adaptive and cross-browser layouts, using in my arsenal such tools as IED WebStorm, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD.
My path front developer began with a love of modern technology and the desire to be a direct participant in technological progress.
In 2018, it is impossible to imagine a person without a tablet computer-smartphone, since our life is in a direct collaboration with various gadgets.

Now the Internet is not just a whim, but a lifestyle. We work, we search for information, we communicate, we share knowledge, we study, we make purchases, we do our favorite things - all this we do with the help of the Internet. And it's so cool!
I like my work, that's why I'm always up-to-date with current trends, constantly developing and studying.
When creating a site layout, I first of all think about how interesting, understandable and accessible it will be to people. I create a tool for interaction between the Internet and the user. That's why I try to make the layout as accessible, understandable and logical as possible.
